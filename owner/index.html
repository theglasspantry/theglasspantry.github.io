Hi, My name is Jenna.

Hi. My name is Jenna and I want to make your zero waste journey a little less difficult. When I started trying to reduce waste in my own home, I found that the bulk section was an awesome place to hang out. Buying in bulk allowed me to easily avoid packaging, get the quantities I wanted, and save money. I was all like, “This isn’t so hard, my garbage can will be history in no time!” But I quickly realized that bulk shopping wasn’t that easy. I couldn’t find everything I needed in one store or even at all in some cases. The tare process was cumbersome and stores would discontinue bulk items as soon as I found them. Determined to leave a better world for my kid, I decided to create the place I was looking for.

My goal is to provide you with all of your household and pantry staples in one place. I also want to help local suppliers and small businesses increase their reach and eliminate waste in the supply chain. This is a community effort. Together, we can make a difference for ourselves, our kids, and our planet by slowing down and rethinking how we shop. I’m so excited to meet you, learn your story, and help support YOUR mission in this world as well!
