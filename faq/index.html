Frequently Asked Questions

  • How does it work?

    Buying in bulk means you get to choose the quantity of a product you need and pay by weight. Dispense into a container of your choosing and pay only for what you need. Feel free to bring your own clean containers to fill or use what we have in the store.

  • Who are your suppliers?

    Our goal is to use local and organic suppliers whenever possible. Products come to us from businesses commitmented to low waste, sustainability, and quality products. We are proud to support midwestern farmers and other small businesses working hard to make a difference.

  • What if i don't see the product i need?

    Let us know! We stock products based on your feedback so if you are looking for something specific, please let us know so we can meet your needs.

  • What if i don't see the product i need?

    You can use any clean container to fill with products. Bags, jars, and tupperware are easy to fill. If the mouth of your container is small, make sure to use a funnel when filling from the bulk bins.
